Productive Harvesting Locations

Ogasawara Islands, Japan

Here you can find Sheepshead Wrasse, Fairy Wrasse, plus many more unique species.

Apalachicola Bay, Florida

In this vast bay, you can find varieties of redfish, flounder, and much more to fill your tank!

Cook Islands, Australia

In this amazing habitat, there are multiple types of parrotfish, clownfish, & triggerfish.

Amazon River Basin, Brazil

The amazon holds amazing fish such as the arrowanna, araipaima, and the beautiful peacock bass.
